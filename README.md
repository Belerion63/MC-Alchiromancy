# Alchiromancy

> **Game design document for a potential Minecraft mod.**
> This is *not* a mod (yet) — it's a collection of design notes describing a magic & alchemy system that may, one day, become one.

A playthrough-spanning system built on two opposing forces — **Magic** (create / transform) and **Alchemy** (transmute / amplify) — that must serve one another. The player who balances both is an **Alchiromancer**.

Start with [`EN/Gameplay/Alchemy.md`](EN/Gameplay/Alchemy.md) and [`EN/Gameplay/Magic.md`](EN/Gameplay/Magic.md), and keep [`EN/Glossary.md`](EN/Glossary.md) open beside them.

## Status

🚧 **Design phase.** No code, no playable build. Everything here is concept, lore and mechanics on paper.

## A note on the files

These notes are **written by the author in French** (`FR/` folder). The English files (`EN/` folder) are **AI-translated** for GitHub and may contain wording inaccuracies — the French versions are the source of truth.

A few terms recur throughout the notes but don't have their own page yet — **Portal Tier**, **Portal Alteration**, **Guardian**, **Alternative Monster**, and **Alchiromancy** itself as an overview. They're listed in the [Glossary](EN/Glossary.md) and left as plain text (not links) until they're written up.

`La magie.md` / `Magic.md` and `La magie et L'alchimie.md` / `Magic and Alchemy.md` predate the detailed Gameplay notes and may be **outdated**.
