Transmutation is the best-known form of alchemy. It's an equivalent exchange between our world and the Alternate World.

It's a stable science with strict rules. If the exchange isn't equivalent, nothing happens.

Introduction to alchemy:

First of all you need to set up the workspace. You need the means of communication from our world to the Alternate World: the Ender Pearl.

When you see an Enderman teleport, it's actually crossing from our world to the Alternate World. It's the one and only entity that belongs to no world — a traveller between dimensions, an observer.

Once you've got an Ender Pearl, you cook it in a furnace to obtain a Cracked Ender Pearl.

The cracked pearl is ready to be reworked: now place it at the forge with an [[Alchemist's Hammer]]. This gives you an Ender Lens.

Then, with the Ender Lens, you can craft an Alchemist's Monocle:

The Monocle lets you glimpse certain elements of the Alternate World.

Now you'll go looking for "Alternate Wells". These are like black holes that rarely appear in our world. They let you perform alchemy rituals.

Once a well is found (a sort of sphere swirling on itself), you must stabilize it before you can start.

To do so you'll have to enter the portal, then clear a small dungeon and defeat its boss (alternate dimension). This stabilizes the portal and makes it usable.

Once the boss is beaten, you return to the portal, which has become a much more stable structure (a portal structure, with custom blocks).

Now you just throw resources through the portal to perform an exchange.

To start a ritual you throw in an Ender Pearl; to end a ritual (confirm or cancel) you simply right-click.
