**Oneiric Dew:** a rain that doesn't wet, doesn't put out fire, and doesn't water plants. But it does make every invisible entity visible and drives monsters away. This rain lasts 2 days.

Cost: 15 [Vital Energy](../Gameplay/Vital%20Energy.md).

How to cast this spell: Lapis Lazuli in right hand + Water Vial in left hand | right-click.
Animation: hands bring the 2 items together, the lapis starts giving off fine rain particles, the water vial turns blue. Then "release" — rain appears in a 32-block radius around the player.

Catalyst: [Aquatic Condenser](../Catalysts/Aquatic%20Condenser.md).

Catalyst effect: the rain suddenly stops, a thunderclap rings out, then the rain falls again giving off magenta particles on the ground. The rain now extinguishes all fires (except Oneiric Fire) and applies a passive growth effect to plants.

This rain lasts a full day.

Cost: 25 [Vital Energy](../Gameplay/Vital%20Energy.md).
