**Absolute Freeze:** summons a whirlwind of snow at the targeted location. The summoning mage can ride it to be launched 30 blocks into the air, though they'll take 3 hearts of damage. It stays here for 4 days.

Cost: 10 [Vital Energy](../Gameplay/Vital%20Energy.md).

How to cast this spell: Blue Ice in right hand + [Wind Vial](../Crafting/Wind%20Vial.md) in left hand | right-click.
Animation: hands bring the 2 items together, the blue ice starts to tremble and give off snow particles. Then "release" — a whirlwind of snow appears on the targeted block.

Catalyst: [Cryogenic Cooler](../Catalysts/Cryogenic%20Cooler.md).

Catalyst effect: the snow whirlwind bursts, then progressively changes the biome into tundra.

Cost: 30 [Vital Energy](../Gameplay/Vital%20Energy.md).
