**Flamelet:** a flickering flame suspended in the air. Without a catalyst, it only lights up its surroundings the same way a lighter's flame would. It can stay lit for entire days.

Cost: 5 [Vital Energy](../Gameplay/Vital%20Energy.md).

How to cast this spell: Ruby in right hand + ash in left hand | right-click.
Animation: hands bring the 2 items together, the ruby starts to tremble and give off sparks, the ash turns red. Then "release" — the oneiric flame appears 1 block away, at a height of 2 blocks.

Catalyst: [Pyric Primer](../Catalysts/Pyric%20Primer.md).

Catalyst effect: the flamelet starts to flicker, then swirl, before settling beneath the camera's cursor. A right-click sends the flamelet crashing down wherever the cursor is aiming, then an [Oneiric Fire](../Crafting/Oneiric%20Fire.md) appears at that spot.

Cost: 10 [Vital Energy](../Gameplay/Vital%20Energy.md).
