The siphon is a fairly simple redstone-powered machine that pumps alternative energy from a portal and sends it through a [Syllarite Chute](Syllarite%20Chute.md).

|                                        |                                                            |                   |

Visual:

A machine clad in [syllarite](Syllarite.md); the sides let you see the pump's fill gauge. A red indicator light on the left blinks like a redstone clock, and on the right a lever turns the pump on or off. On top you can see a piston "pumping" using a diamond block. Inside, the diamond block rises to draw energy in, then lowers to push the energy toward the chute.
