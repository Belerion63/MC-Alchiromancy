The Stasis Stone is a fascinating block. Alchemists use it to imprison fire and keep it under control. It can imprison normally unstable elements in a perfectly stable state, making it the perfect tool for controlling the temperature of a cauldron or a crucible.

To create a Stasis Stone, you must cast the [Absolute Freeze](../Spells/Absolute%20Freeze.md) spell on a magma block. The spell's nature can be inconvenient — you can always catalyze it if you don't want to wait.
