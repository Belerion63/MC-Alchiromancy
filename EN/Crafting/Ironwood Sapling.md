Ironwood is a fairly common tree, the equivalent of oak in the Alternate World. But during its crossing, with iron added to it, its nature is altered, which lets it grow iron from its foliage.

| [Blood](Blood.md) Vial | Iron Block   | Blood Vial |
| ------------------------ | ------------ | ---------- |
| Iron Block                | Oak Sapling  | Iron Block |
| Blood                     | Seed         | Blood Vial |

Ironwood is planted on stone, and doesn't grow in light.
