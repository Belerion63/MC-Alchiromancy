Syllarite is one of the first alchiromancy processes you perform. You incorporate magic during a gold alchemy transmutation.

To do this, cast the [Flamelet](../Spells/Flamelet.md) spell (without a catalyst) directly into the portal. The portal should change color. Once that's done, simply apply the same process as for gold — throw in your 2 copper and 1 iron (ingots or blocks). The portal will agitate, then give you your syllarite.

(A bright midnight-blue ingot.)
