Impregnation has succeeded, but as every alchemist knows, our world follows rules. And you broke them.

Your impregnated item/entity is agitated — it's overflowing with energy, and that energy wants only one thing: to return to its own world. You have no choice; you'll have to reseal the portal you opened if you want to avoid catastrophe.

It's simple: to open the portal, you killed its guardian. So you'll have to supply it with a new one!

To do this, you'll have to re-enter the portal and supply any living (vanilla) entity into the dimension. It doesn't matter which one, and it doesn't matter how, as long as an entity ends up in the dimension you'll have succeeded (spawn egg, walking through the portal, a golem, a wither, a dog, a cat, a chicken egg, etc...).

Upon entering, you'll land directly in the boss room. As soon as an entity sets foot there, it will automatically be absorbed at the center of the room and a transformation will begin. You have 5 minutes (boss bar) to escape (obstacle course + enemies).

If you fail to get out of the portal, you'll be teleported straight back into the guardian's room. You'll have no choice but to reopen the portal if you want to survive.
In that case, if the portal is reopened, simply put, you must restart the Lock stage.

If you manage to get out of the portal, the Lock stage is complete and your item / entity is fully amplified.

If you die inside the dungeon, your gear will be kept by the dungeon's guardian and your Lock will not be validated.
