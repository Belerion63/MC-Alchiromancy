Amplification is a radical way to grow in power, but it's costly and dangerous. In exchange for a large quantity of resources, you amplify the capabilities of an item or an entity. It's an unstable process that can create collisions between the normal world and the Alternate World.

Amplification unfolds across 4 key stages: Absorption, Stabilization, Impregnation, and the Lock. Each of these stages turns out to be relatively risky and unstable — a single mistake and... disaster. Anything with a stat can be amplified: equipment, living beings, consumables.

The power and difficulty of the amplification depend on the Portal Tier.

Here is the detail of each stage.

[Absorption](Absorption.md): once finished, the Energy can stay in the siphon for as long as you want — just be careful not to break it while it's full.

[Stabilization](Stabilization.md): once finished, the Energy can stay in the crucible for as long as you want — just be careful not to break it while it's full.

[Impregnation](Impregnation.md): once finished, the item/entity must absolutely not receive any interaction.

[The Lock](The%20Lock.md): the final stage to finally complete the amplification process.

The goal is for every "end game" item to have an amplified variant.

All entities should have an amplified variant that provides either resources from the mod, an interesting base defense, or an even more convenient means of transportation.
