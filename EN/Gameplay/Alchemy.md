Alchemy is a form of magic, but a different one — it's an exchange. Everything has a balance, an opposite. Our world doesn't escape that rule; imagine seeing everything in negative. That's what the Alternate World is — as if, for a thing to exist, it must also exist in a contrary state.

Forget everything you know about our world; here the rules have nothing in common. Fire isn't fire, water isn't water, earth isn't earth... But no matter, no one has ever actually gone there. All we do is trade.

We use the Alternate World in 2 ways.

[Transmutation](Transmutation.md): in exchange for resources equivalent to the request, the Alternate World provides us with an equivalent from its world.

[Amplification](Amplification.md): we imbue an object or entity with alternative energy. It's a very powerful process that can work miracles — a simple iron pickaxe can become tougher than diamond.

The problem is that in alchemy, nothing is free. But amplification gives nothing back in exchange. When you amplify something, you go against the world's logic, and that brings about problems the alchemist must confront during the process.
