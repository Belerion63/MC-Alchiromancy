Absorption lets you draw Alternative Energy from the portal into your container. To begin, you'll need an [Alchemist's Siphon](../Crafting/Alchemist's%20Siphon.md), an [Alchemist's Crucible](../Crafting/Alchemist's%20Crucible.md), and a [Syllarite Chute](../Crafting/Syllarite%20Chute.md).

Once you have them, place the siphon adjacent to the portal, then connect it to the crucible with one or more syllarite chutes.

Now that everything is in place to prepare the absorption, get ready — once it starts, there's no turning back.

Activating the lever starts the siphon. Stand facing the portal.

The siphon emits a sound and particles; every X seconds it will suddenly stop — this means the portal is agitating and an alteration is about to occur.

To prevent it, simply hit the visual effect that appears somewhere near the portal (3-block radius, 3-block height). If you succeed, the absorption resumes; if you fail.. → Portal Alteration.

This takes roughly 1 minute to fill the siphon. The mini-game's difficulty and duration depend entirely on the Portal Tier.

Once finished, the siphon stops on its own.
