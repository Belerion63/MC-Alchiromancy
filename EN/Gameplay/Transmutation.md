Transmutation is the best-known form of alchemy. It's a fair exchange between our world and the Alternate World.

It's a stable science with strict rules. If the exchange isn't fair, then nothing happens.

Introduction to alchemy:

Before anything else, you need to set up your workspace. You need a means of communication from our world to the Alternate World: the Ender Pearl.

When you see an Enderman teleport, it's actually passing from our world into the Alternate World. It's the one and only entity that belongs to neither world — a traveler between the different dimensions, an observer.

The monocle lets you glimpse certain elements of the Alternate World.

Now we're going to go searching for "Alternate Wells." These are like black holes that rarely appear in our world. They let you cast alchemy rituals.

Once a well is found (a sort of sphere swirling on itself), it must be stabilized before it can be used.

To do this, you'll need to enter the portal, clear a small dungeon, and defeat its boss (in the alternate dimension) — this will stabilize the portal and allow it to be used.

Once the boss is defeated, you return in front of the portal, which has become a much more stable structure (a portal structure, with 4x4 custom blocks).

Now all you have to do is throw resources through the portal to carry out an exchange.

To start a ritual, you throw in an Ender Pearl.
