Once stabilization is complete, we'll be able to impregnate our item / entity with alternative energy.

Since the energy is stabilized, it can be handled without too much danger — let's collect it with an [Oneiric Glass Vial](../Crafting/Oneiric%20Glass%20Vial.md). You might think that sounds like very little, but no — alternative energy doesn't follow the rules of our world, it doesn't have the same density, the same fluidity, the same atoms. That said, the quantity in your [Alternative Energy Vial](../Crafting/Alternative%20Energy%20Vial.md) is equivalent to a full bucket.

> [!warning]
> Careful, your vial will consume itself within 24h. At that point, the effects are dangerous and can once again trigger a Portal Alteration.

There are 2 possible cases for impregnation: an entity or an object.

An entity: simple — confine the desired entity *(detail left blank in the original — the exact containment method is still undecided)* and pour the bottle's contents over it (careful, once poured you can't put it back in the bottle, it's too late!), then use the [Alchemist's Knife](../Crafting/Alchemist's%20Knife.md) to make cuts on it (right-click).

An object: place your item on a [Syllarite](../Crafting/Syllarite.md) anvil (same crafting pattern as a regular anvil). Then, with the vial in your left hand and the [Alchemist's Hammer](../Crafting/Alchemist's%20Hammer.md) in your right hand, right-click repeatedly.

Each right-click triggers a clap of thunder and the portal agitates — pay close attention, because doing it too many times will have devastating effects.

While the portal is agitating, do not right-click under any circumstances, or you risk causing a Portal Alteration. While the portal is calm, you have a 2-second window to right-click again, or risk breaking the item and the portal losing a Portal Tier.

Each right-click raises the entity's or item's impregnation level by one.

Once you reach the maximum impregnation threshold, a new sound will play and the vial will shatter. The item or entity will be visually unstable (entity: trembling, particles, sounds.. | item: vibration, particles, sounds..).

From that point on, Impregnation is complete.
