Absorption is finished — we're now going to be able to send the alternative energy to the [Alchemist's Crucible](../Crafting/Alchemist's%20Crucible.md).

But this too is a sensitive step — the crucible must be at the perfect temperature throughout stabilization.

Place a [Stasis Stone](../Crafting/Stasis%20Stone.md) under the crucible, then summon an [Oneiric Fire](../Crafting/Oneiric%20Fire.md) on it.

To start stabilization, right-click the crucible — the lens will open and let the alternative energy in.

From that point, night will suddenly fall, no matter the time of day. The game clock will freeze and a 5-minute counter (boss bar) will start. During these 5 minutes, the portal will agitate and spawn Alternative Monsters nearby. Their number and power depend entirely on the difficulty of the Portal Tier.

At the end of the 5 minutes, if you survived, the stage is validated. Otherwise, the crucible will have been emptied of all its energy.

Either way, a burst of particles erupts from the portal at that moment, time returns to its initial state, and the game clock resumes as if nothing happened.

Stabilization is complete.
