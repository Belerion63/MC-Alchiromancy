**Flamelet:** a flickering flame suspended in the air. Without a catalyst it only lights up the surroundings, much like a lighter's flame. It can stay present for days on end.

Cost: 5 [Vital Energy](Vital%20Energy.md).

How to cast: Ruby in right hand + Ash in left hand | right-click.
Animation: the hands bring the two items together, the Ruby starts to tremble and throw sparks, the ash reddens. Then "release" — a oneiric flame appears 1 block away, at a height of 2 blocks.

Catalyst: [Pyric Primer](Pyric%20Primer.md).

Catalyst effect: the Flamelet flickers, then swirls before settling beneath the camera cursor. A right-click sends the Flamelet crashing where the cursor aims, then spawns an [Oneiric Fire](Oneiric%20Fire.md) at that spot.

Cost: 10 [Vital Energy](Vital%20Energy.md).
