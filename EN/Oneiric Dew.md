**Oneiric Dew:** a rain that doesn't wet, doesn't put out fire and doesn't water plants. But it reveals any invisible entity and keeps monsters away. This rain lasts 2 days.

Cost: 15 [[Vital Energy]].

How to cast: Lapis Lazuli in right hand + Water Vial in left hand | right-click.
Animation: the hands bring the two items together, the Lapis starts to emit fine rain particles, the water vial turns blue. Then "release" — the rain appears in a 32-block radius around the player.

Catalyst: [[Aquatic Condenser]].

Catalyst effect: the rain stops suddenly, a thunderclap rings out, then the rain falls again, emitting magenta particles on the ground. The rain now extinguishes all fires (except Oneiric Fire) and applies a passive growth effect to plants.

This rain lasts 1 full day.

Cost: 25 [[Vital Energy]].
