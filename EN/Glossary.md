# Glossary — Alchiromancy

Reference for the coined terms used across the design notes. The **FR** column gives the original French term, so translations stay consistent as the project grows.

> [!warning] Conceptual / possibly outdated
> Three early notes — [[Concept]], [[Magic]] and [[Magic and Alchemy]] — predate the detailed design and may no longer be accurate. Terms that only appear there (e.g. **alchemical fire**) are marked *(conceptual)* below.

## Disciplines & forces

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Alchiromancy** | Alchiromancie | The mod itself, and the combined practice of magic + alchemy. |
| **Alchiromancer** | Alchiromancien | A practitioner who balances both forces to draw on them endlessly. |
| **Magic** | Magie | The *creation* force. Produces intangible, [[Magic\|oneiric]] things superimposed on reality. |
| **Alchemy** | Alchimie | The *exchange* force. Reshapes and concretizes matter through the Alternate World. See [[Alchemy]]. |
| **Transmutation** | Transmutation | Strict, rule-bound equivalent exchange. See [[Transmutation]]. |
| **Amplification** | Amplification | Rule-less imbuing of an object/entity with alternate energy. See [[Amplification]]. |

## World & concepts

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Alternate World** | Monde alternatif | The contrary/"negative" reality used for every exchange. |
| **Oneiric (state)** | Onirique | The intangible magical form of a spell; superimposed on reality, cannot interact with it. |
| **Casting Matrix** | Matrice de lancement | The right-hand + left-hand item combination that casts a spell. |
| **Catalyst** | Catalyseur | A stone that translates a spell into our world's logic, giving it consistency. See [[Spells and Catalysts]]. |
| **Alternate Well** | Puits alternatif | A rare rift (swirling sphere) where alchemy rituals are performed. |

## Resources & stats

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Vital Energy** | Énergie vitale | Stamina-type resource for magic (50, upgradable to 100). See [[Vital Energy]]. |
| **Blood** | Sang | Exchange-balancing resource, drawn at the cost of hearts. See [[Blood]]. |

## Spells

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Flamelet** | Flamiche | Basic light spell; catalyzed into Oneiric Fire. See [[Flamelet]]. |
| **Oneiric Dew** | Rosée Onirique | Rain spell that reveals the invisible and wards off monsters. See [[Oneiric Dew]]. |
| **Oneiric Fire** | Feu onirique | Catalyzed turquoise-magenta fire that burns only hostile entities. See [[Oneiric Fire]]. |
