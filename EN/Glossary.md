# Glossary — Alchiromancy

Reference for the coined terms used across the design notes. The **FR** column gives the original French term, so translations stay consistent as the project grows.

> [!warning] Conceptual / possibly outdated
> Three early notes — [Concept](Concept.md), [Magic](Magic.md) and [Magic and Alchemy](Magic%20and%20Alchemy.md) — predate the detailed design and may no longer be accurate. Terms that only appear there (e.g. **alchemical fire**) are marked *(conceptual)* below.

## Disciplines & forces

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Alchiromancy** | Alchiromancie | The mod itself, and the combined practice of magic + alchemy. |
| **Alchiromancer** | Alchiromancien | A practitioner who balances both forces to draw on them endlessly. |
| **Magic** | Magie | The *creation* force. Produces intangible, [oneiric](Magic.md) things superimposed on reality. |
| **Alchemy** | Alchimie | The *exchange* force. Reshapes and concretizes matter through the Alternate World. See [Alchemy](Alchemy.md). |
| **Transmutation** | Transmutation | Strict, rule-bound equivalent exchange. See [Transmutation](Transmutation.md). |
| **Amplification** | Amplification | Rule-less imbuing of an object/entity with alternate energy. See [Amplification](Amplification.md). |

## World & concepts

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Alternate World** | Monde alternatif | The contrary/"negative" reality used for every exchange. |
| **Oneiric (state)** | Onirique | The intangible magical form of a spell; superimposed on reality, cannot interact with it. |
| **Casting Matrix** | Matrice de lancement | The right-hand + left-hand item combination that casts a spell. |
| **Catalyst** | Catalyseur | A stone that translates a spell into our world's logic, giving it consistency. See [Spells and Catalysts](Spells%20and%20Catalysts.md). |
| **Alternate Well** | Puits alternatif | A rare rift (swirling sphere) where alchemy rituals are performed. |

## Resources & stats

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Vital Energy** | Énergie vitale | Stamina-type resource for magic (50, upgradable to 100). See [Vital Energy](Vital%20Energy.md). |
| **Blood** | Sang | Exchange-balancing resource, drawn at the cost of hearts. See [Blood](Blood.md). |

## Spells

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Flamelet** | Flamiche | Basic light spell; catalyzed into Oneiric Fire. See [Flamelet](Flamelet.md). |
| **Oneiric Dew** | Rosée Onirique | Rain spell that reveals the invisible and wards off monsters. See [Oneiric Dew](Oneiric%20Dew.md). |
| **Oneiric Fire** | Feu onirique | Catalyzed turquoise-magenta fire that burns only hostile entities. See [Oneiric Fire](Oneiric%20Fire.md). |

## Catalysts, materials & alloys

| Term (EN) | FR | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Pyric Primer** | Amorce pyrique | Minor fire catalyst. See [Pyric Primer](Pyric%20Primer.md). |
| **Aquatic Condenser** | Condenseur Aquatique | Minor water catalyst. See [Aquatic Condenser](Aquatic%20Condenser.md). |
