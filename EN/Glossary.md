# Glossary

Every coined term used across these notes, with its French original. The French notes are the source of truth — if a translation here ever feels off, check the original first.

## Core concepts

| English | French original | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Alchiromancy | *Alchiromancie* | The mod's core discipline: balancing Magic and Alchemy. Not yet expanded into its own note. |
| Alchiromancer | *Alchiromancien* | A player who masters both Magic and Alchemy. |
| Magic | *Magie* — [Magic](Gameplay/Magic.md) | The *create* force. Intangible until catalyzed. |
| Alchemy | *Alchimie* — [Alchemy](Gameplay/Alchemy.md) | The *exchange* force, channeled through the Alternate World. |
| Alternate World | *Monde alternatif* | The "negative" counterpart of reality that alchemy trades with. |
| Oneiric (state) | *Onirique* | The intangible, catalyst-less state of a freshly cast spell or material. |
| Portal Tier | *Tier de portail* | Drives the difficulty/power of Absorption, Stabilization, Impregnation and the Lock. Not yet documented. |
| Portal Alteration | *Altération de portail* | The failure consequence across several Amplification stages. Not yet documented. |
| Guardian | *Gardien* | The entity guarding a stabilized portal; killed to open it, must be replaced during [The Lock](Gameplay/The%20Lock.md). |
| Alternative Monster | *Monstre alternatif* | Hostile spawns during [Stabilization](Gameplay/Stabilization.md). |
